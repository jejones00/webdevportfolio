Introduction:

Born: June 26, 1929 - Died: June 26, 2020


Milton Glaser is amongst one of the most iconic graphic designers to date. The New York native was the creator of the well-known and well-loved I heart NY design that has withheld the test of time and is known by many. He actually created the design pro-bono, and has even created renditions of this piece! Glaser opened Push Pin Studios in 1954 in New York, building a successful firm where his creativity alongside others could blossom. Many of Glaser;s designs have a similar stylized approach, often times bold black images or text are paired with electric, and bright color choices.




A few famous design pieces of his work: "Bob Dylan", "Big Nude (Yellow)" "I Love NY More Than Ever"
