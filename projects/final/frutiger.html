Introduction:

Born: June 26, 1929 - Died: June 26, 2020


Adrian Frutiger is one of the most well known and acclaimed tyoeface designer to have ever graced the design world. Frutiger dedicated almost all of his life to creating unique, humanist san-serif typefaces that were eye-catching while being digestible to the public eye. Non-design people can even recognize his work due to its popularity. One of Frutiger's most known typefaes is Frutiger and it can be found in airports all across the world.
